ABOUT THE PROJECT

Built to Make the First Day Simpler

Junior's Help Desk is a student-created prototype designed to help first-year students navigate the university building on their first day. Scan, log in, understand your classroom code, and find important locations.

This prototype is a navigation assistance concept and does not replace official university notices or information systems.
